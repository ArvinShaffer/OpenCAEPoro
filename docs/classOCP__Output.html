Detailed Description

OCP_Output manages different kinds of ways to output. the most commonly used is summary file. which usually give the information of bulks and wells in each timestep, such as average bulks pressure, oil production rate of wells. if other information at critical time is interested in, you can chose the PRT file(to do). also, some infomation will be printed on the screen at the critical time to make sure the program is at the right way.
